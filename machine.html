Jack F4 Sewing Machine - Complete Guide

High-Speed Direct Drive Motor

The Jack F4 is equipped with a **direct drive motor**, ensuring smooth operation, reduced vibration, and energy efficiency. This motor eliminates the need for a clutch motor, making it quieter and more user-friendly.

Adjustable Needle Position

With the **automatic needle positioning feature**, the Jack F4 allows you to set the needle to stop in the **up** or **down** position, making sewing more precise and reducing hand movement.

Energy-Saving Technology

The machine consumes **71% less power** compared to traditional sewing machines, thanks to its advanced **servo motor**. This feature reduces electricity costs and makes the machine eco-friendly.

Built-in LED Light

The Jack F4 comes with an **adjustable LED light**, providing better visibility while working on dark fabrics. The brightness can be customized according to your needs.

Reverse Sewing & Auto-Lock Stitch

With the **reverse stitching** button, users can reinforce stitches at the beginning and end of a seam. This ensures durability and prevents fabric from unraveling.

Digital Display & Speed Control

The **digital control panel** allows easy speed adjustment (up to 5,000 stitches per minute). Beginners can start at a lower speed and increase as they gain confidence.
